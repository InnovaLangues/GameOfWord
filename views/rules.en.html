Greetings Wise One, welcome in Game of Words!

It’s time to show off your talents!

Game of Word offers an asynchronous online avatar of guessing game, reusing game mechanics from ‘Taboo’, created in 1990 by Brian Hersch and Bernard Bougrèle, and published by Hasbro. The purpose of the game is to make other players guess words in a limited time. Be careful, on each card are written forbidden words. Words of the same ‘family’ are also forbidden.

Augur

The Oracle left you some clues, would you find out the word?

A word will be randomly selected (given you were neither Oracle, nor Druid for it). Check on you headphones or speaker and a countdown starts. You will get a certain amount of time (based on the level of difficulty) to enter the word to guess in the blank field. Be careful, spelling mistakes will not be accepted (including on diacritics)! you are all supposed to be wise! You can submit as many words as you want during the listening. If you find out the word, you will get points. If you do not, you and the Oracle you have been listening to will lose points. No pressure at all…


Scoring

Your growth shall follow complex and intertwined incantations…

The scoring of the game follows a formula that depends on your level, the level of the card and the difficulty you chose. A basic stake is chosen by you in terms of difficulty and adds certain constraints (more or less forbidden words, more or less time) and is associated to stakes. The more difficult, the more you can earn… and the more you can lose. Then each card has a difficulty, if you play a card difficult considering your level, the risks are low, you will win more if you win and lose less if you lose. With easier cards you are bound to win easy points… but if you lose, you will lose more.
